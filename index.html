Speedlimit 88 GPS Speedometer

Since 2015, Speedlimit 88 has been the go-to GPS speedometer for iPhone and iPad, featuring an iconic Big. Red. Circle. display inspired by Australian speed limit signs. Whether you're driving, cycling, jet skiing, or even taking the luge down a mountain, this app delivers your GPS-detected speed in a bold, instantly readable format that works anywhere under the sky.

Customize your experience with day and night themes that adapt to any lighting condition—just tap the speed display to toggle between them. The built-in GPS accuracy indicator shifts from green to orange to red, giving you real-time feedback on location precision so you always know when your speed reading is most reliable.

Unlock the optional map feature with a one-time in-app purchase (no recurring subscription) to view your location, explore points of interest, and toggle between 2D and 3D modes. The map works seamlessly alongside the speedometer, letting you browse around while keeping your speed front and center, or minimize it for a larger map view with just a tap.
