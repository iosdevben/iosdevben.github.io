Harken Offline Music Player

The fastest way to rediscover your music.

Beautiful & Intuitive

Experience a beautiful, gesture-driven music player designed to put your entire music collection just a tap away. Navigate your library effortlessly with intuitive controls that respond to simple gestures, making it faster than ever to find and play exactly what you want to hear.

Make Harken truly your own with customizable light and dark themes, accessible font options, and innovative bottom-based navigation that keeps all controls comfortably within reach. Every detail is designed to enhance your listening experience while reflecting your personal style.

Rediscover the hidden gems in your music collection with powerful sorting options that help you find songs you haven't heard in ages. Shuffle albums or tracks by artist, composer, or genre, and explore your library in entirely new ways that bring fresh life to your favorite music.

Key Features

Gesture Controls. Control playback with simple gestures anywhere on the screen—perfect for driving or when your device is docked.

Bottom Navigation. All primary controls are at the bottom of the screen, so you don't have to do gymnastics to reach buttons.

Powerful Sorting. Sort your library alphabetically, by date added, recently played, play count, rating, or year, to quickly find music you haven't listened to in ages.

On-the-Go Playlists. Create playlists from albums, artists, genres, songs, or podcast episodes with ease.

Customizable Themes. Choose between dark or light themes, full-screen or small cover art, and even your favorite font.

iPad Support. Harken 5.0 brings full iPad support with a beautiful interface optimized for the larger screen.

What's New in Version 5.0

This is a huge update with lots of small UI changes that make Harken easier and more pleasurable to use. The biggest change is that Harken now fully supports iPad too.

iPad support added. Harken now runs beautifully on iPad with a fully optimized interface designed specifically for the larger screen.

More space for albums. The UI has been refreshed throughout to give your album artwork and library more room to breathe.

New navigation menus. Library mode and sorting options have been redesigned with clearer, more intuitive menu interfaces.

Sort by year. Your music library can now be sorted by release year, making it easy to explore your collection chronologically.

See release years. The Now Playing screen now displays the year each song was released, adding context to your listening experience.

Metadata on albums. Sort information is now visible on every album when browsing, so you always know how your library is organized.

Accessible text sizes. Font sizes throughout the app have been updated to be more readable and meet accessibility standards.

Coming soon... We're working on exciting new features that will make Harken even better. Stay tuned for updates.

More features ahead. Additional improvements and capabilities are in development and will be released in future updates.
